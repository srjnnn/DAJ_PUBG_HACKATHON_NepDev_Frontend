# Mero Sathi (मेरो साथी) – You are not alone. We hear you.

##  Problem Statement  
- Rising cases of **depression, anxiety, and suicide** in Nepal.  
- **Stigma** prevents people from openly seeking help.  
- **Accessibility gap**: Limited availability of mental health professionals, especially in rural areas.  
- Existing **NGOs/INGOs** lack effective bridges to connect with those in need.  

---

##  Our Solution – Mero Sathee  
A **Telemedicine & Health-Tech platform** dedicated to mental health in Nepal.  

###  Key Features  
- **Anonymous Access** – Seek help without revealing identity.  
- **VOIP Integration** – Secure, anonymous voice calls with trained volunteers & counselors.  
- **AI-powered Mental Wellbeing Test** – Quick self-checkup using trained models & LLMs.  
- **Bridge to NGOs/INGOs** – Connect users with existing organizations for professional help.  

---

##  Tech Stack  
- **Frontend:** React.js  
- **Backend:** Node.js + Express  
- **Database/Auth:** Supabase  
- **Anonymous VOIP:** In-house system

---

##  Why Mero Sathee?  
Mental health is often neglected due to stigma, lack of awareness, and limited access.  
**Mero Sathee** acts as a **trusted friend** — offering anonymity, guidance, and a bridge to professional care.  

---

##  Profit & Sustainability Model  
- **Volunteer Model (1:10):** Free for all — 1 volunteer supports up to 10 people.  
- **Premium 1-to-1 Counseling:** Paid sessions with certified professionals.  
- Revenue will be used for **platform development & maintenance**.  

---

##  Future Plans  
- Complete in-house **VOIP system**.(currently ~60% complete)  
- Introduce **paid professional 1-to-1 sessions**.  
- Collaborate with **NGOs, INGOs, and hospitals**.  
- Add **Nepali + regional language support** for wider reach.
